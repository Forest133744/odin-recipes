Gulas

Description

Gulas is a stewy dish made mostly from mixed meats and a lot of paprika

A very strong on stomach dish, served often as last meals for soldier who are about to be deployed

Ingredients

  • Mixed meat
  • Garlic
  • Peper
  • Salt
  • 4l of water

Prep

  1. Put everything to boil for a long time
  2. Mix it a few times so meat doesnt stick to the bottom of the dish
  3. Enjoy your Gulas
