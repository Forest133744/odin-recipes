Cevapi

Description

Cevapi are one of the best balkan dishes ever made, very meaty very nutrios and very tasty

Also one of the most favorite food in Serbia

Ingredients

  • Mixed meat
  • Garlic
  • Peper
  • Salt
  • Juice

Prep

  1. Unfreeze the meat
  2. Put it into oven for 2-2.5 hrs
  3. Enjoy your Cevapi
